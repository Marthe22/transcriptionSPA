Policy T1

Permission: T1-P1
A permission to 
	derive data for internal use from
	and run automated trading over 
		static end of day Eurodollar Futures Contract EoD
		and dynamic Eurodollar Futures Contract
			when delivered less than 10 minutes  after origination
		and dynamic Eurodollar Futures Contract
			when delivered less than 8 hours  after origination and when delivered Greater than or equal to 10 minutes  after origination
 	when the end recipient is internal
 	if
 		use is reported 
 		per device
 		and 
 		monthly 
 	and if 
 		attribution is stated
 	and if 
 		payment is made 
 		in United States dollar and the payment amount is 1150.0 and monthly
